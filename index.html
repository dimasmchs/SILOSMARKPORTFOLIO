About My Creative Work
Beyond structural engineering, I channel creativity through video editing and graphic design. I've developed a strong eye for visual storytelling — knowing how to pace a video, choose the right transition, and use color to set the mood.

Video Editing: I work primarily in CapCut, producing everything from event documentation reels to short promotional clips. I enjoy the interplay of music, motion text, and color grading to produce content that feels cinematic even at a short runtime.

Poster Design: Using Canva as my primary tool, I design event posters, announcement materials, and social media graphics. I pay close attention to typography hierarchy, color harmony, and layout balance to create visually impactful outputs.

These creative skills complement my engineering work — giving me the ability to communicate technical ideas visually and produce professional presentation materials for projects.

Welcome to the Reinforced Concrete Design (RCD) Tool — your interactive companion for mastering structural concrete design in accordance with the National Structural Code of the Philippines (NSCP 2015). 📋 How to Add Your Video ✕
Option A — YouTube / Google Drive link:
In the HTML, find id="video-container" and replace the placeholder div with:
<iframe width="100%" style="aspect-ratio:16/9" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_ID" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
Option B — Direct MP4 file:
Host your .mp4 (e.g. on Google Drive or postimg) and use:
<video controls style="width:100%;aspect-ratio:16/9;background:#000" src="YOUR_DIRECT_LINK.mp4"></video>
Poster images: In the Poster Gallery tab, upload images to postimg.cc and replace the placeholder divs with <img src="URL">.
